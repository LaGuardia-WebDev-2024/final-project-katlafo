About Me

Hello, reader! I’m an actress who started out on stage and is now stepping into the world of film. I graduated from LaGuardia High School as a drama major, where I spent years diving into scenes, building characters, and learning what it really means to tell a story. I’ve trained extensively in theater and had the chance to work with Katie Cappiello in a class that pushed me to dig deeper and stay honest in every moment.

I’m represented by Rebel Creative Group and Take 3 Talent, and I’m ready to take on new challenges in front of the camera. I’ve played a wide range of roles—from the wild energy of Black Stache to the quiet heartbreak of Nina in The Seagull. I don’t shy away from contrast. I lean into it.

What drives me most is the chance to reach people. I want to move, challenge, and inspire anyone watching. I want to bring light to voices and stories that often get overlooked. Acting lets me connect, and through that connection, I hope to spark something—whether it’s reflection, conversation, or change.
